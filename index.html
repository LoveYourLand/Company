About

Forestry-trained, Raleigh-rooted

Hi, I'm the founder of Canopy & Root. I recently completed my B.S. in Forestry and bring real scientific knowledge of plant health, soil ecosystems, and native Piedmont species to every project — not just a mower and a truck.

I'm passionate about designing yards that thrive long-term, support local pollinators, and work with the NC Piedmont climate rather than against it. Every recommendation I make is grounded in ecology, not just aesthetics.
